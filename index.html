      OEM&ODM SERVICE

      We oem printer manufacturers are able to make one-stop customized services according to the needs of our customers.

      Requirement collecting

      Communicate with customers to collect the customer’s requirements. 

      Engineer drawing

      Xprinter’s engineer drew the design and confirmed with the customer. If adjustments needed, our engineer will change and reconfirm it.

      Motherboard design and manufacture

      After the drawing confirmed, we will sign a contract and letter of authorization. Then we start to make the sample.

      Machine testing

      After the sample finished, Xprinter’s staff will test it and then send it to customers for checking and testing.

      Packing

      Customers take part in the whole testing process and confirm the sample. Then we put it into mass production.
